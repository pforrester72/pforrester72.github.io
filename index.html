paul.forrester@yale.edu am a PhD student in the Philosophy Department at Yale University where I'm advised by Daniel Greco and Stephen Darwall.

Prior to Yale, I double majored in Philosophy and Political Science at Duke University.

My research is in ethics, political philosophy and philosophy of social science. My dissertation is about the institutions that make economic cooperation possible, and how we can both grow the economic pie and divide it more equitably. My work also deeply engages with figures in the history of political economy and philosophy.
